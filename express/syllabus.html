Course Description

This course provides an overview of the fundamental concepts and theories in psychology. Topics include cognitive processes, development, personality, and social psychology. Students will engage in critical thinking and apply psychological principles to real-world situations.

Course Objectives

  • Understand major psychological theories and their applications.
  • Analyze research methods in psychology.
  • Discuss the biological bases of behavior.
  • Evaluate the impact of social and cultural factors on psychology.

Required Texts

Myers, D. G., & DeWall, C. N. (2021). Psychology (12th ed.). Worth Publishers.
